=== Notes ===
: John Cheadle road supervisor, road from Golansville to Chesterfield,never fined.<ref>Source: [[#S11]] </ref>
: John Cheadle granted 400 acres on the south branch of Polecat creekwith Thomas Hackett.
: Bought land from Thomas Carr of St. Margaret's Parish on the upper branches of Polecat Creek (now Stevens Mill Run).
: John Cheadle registered two young slaves he had purchased with the court, Sam & Gilbert, 10 and 11.
