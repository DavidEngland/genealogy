== Biography ==William Southern was born 1575 in Tichfild, Hampshire, England son ofWilliam Southern.<ref>Citation needed</ref>
Said to have married about 1600 in England, Louise "Lou" (unsourced surname) Kincaid. This marriage is unsourced and unconfirmed.
23 May 1609 the Virginia Charter was appended showing William Southernas an Adventurer (Stockholder).<ref>[http://www.let.rug.nl/usa/documents/1600-1650/the-second-virginia-charter-1609.php The Second VirginiaCharter 1609]</ref> Note, this does not necessarily mean he went to the colony. The language in the Charter states:: ... whether they goe in their persons to be planters there in the said plantacion, or whether they goe not, but doe adventure their monyes, goods or chattels, that they shalbe one bodie or comunaltie perpetuall ....
William Southerne did import tobacco from Virginia. == Research Notes ==[[Southernwood-28|William Southernwood]] was added recently as fatherfor William Southern. No documentation is provided for this relationship. The locations given for residence, birth, death of William Southernwood and his daughter Mary (Southernwood) Benton do not in any way match the same information given for William Southern. Nowhere is "Southernwood" given as a variant of Southerne/Sowthern/Sothern, known variant spellings for William Southern. William Southernwood has been removed as father of William Southern.
