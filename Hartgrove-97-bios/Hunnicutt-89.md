[[Hunnicutt-89|Augustine Hunnicutt]] was born about 1662 in Surry County Virginia. He died between March 8 ,1708 and May 2, 1710 in Surry County Virginia. Born to [[Hunnicutt-97|Augustine Hunnicutt]] and [[Carter-4297|Mary Carter]].[[Hunnicutt-89|Augustine]] married a woman named [[Unknown-322865|Margaret]]. Her last name at birth is unproven at this time , but possiblyit was Phillips.  Margaret 's first husband was [[Cooper-6225|John Cooper]]. Their daughter Elizabeth married Edward Rowells , their son was Richard Rowells. Margaret then married [[Hodges-606|John Hodge]].  Her step son James Hodge died young and had no issue.  Margaret 's stepdaughter Mary Hodge married Bray Hargrove (Hargrave). Augustine and Margaret 's children were Katherine Hunnicutt who married Charles Pittand Ann who married Augustine Berryman.
