== Biography ==
Anne was born in 1720. She passed away in 1773.
Anne Cheadle married first, Manoah Chiles after his first wife, Elizabeth died. She raises the six children from Manoah Chiles' first wife Elizabeth.
She has 6 children with her husband totaling 12 children in the household.

Her step-daughter, Elizabeth Chiles marries Carr McGehee.
Manoah Chiles dies, leaving her with 12 children. Anne marries Carr McGehee, after Elizabeth dies.
Anne has four more children with Carr making her the "mother" to 16 children in total. (Bless her.)
