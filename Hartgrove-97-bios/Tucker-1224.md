== Biography ==A previous version of this profile, citing a Rootsweb discussion grouppost that cited an unnamed book (see Research Notes below), claimed that Memory was daughter of [[Tucker-301|William Tucker]] and [[Thompson-1047|Mary Thompson]].  However, William Tucker made his will while Memory was still living; the will specifically references "my three children" and names them. Memory was not included in the list.<ref>Colonial Virginians and Their Maryland Relatives [database on-line]; [https://www.ancestry.com/imageviewer/collections/48239/images/VAMDRelatives-010475-12?ssrc=&backlabel=Return&clickref=1011lj7ySzBx%2C1011lj7ySzBx&adref=&o_xid=01011l4xx5&o_lid=01011l4xx5&o_sch=Affiliate%2BExternal p17] (link for Ancestry.com subscribers)</ref>

== Research Notes ==*I [who?] copied this information off of Rootsweb, someone's post. However they stated they did not  know the name of the book as someone had photo copies of parts of the book.*[http://archiver.rootsweb.ancestry.com/th/read/SOUTHERN/1999-12/0944731656 RootsWeb post] However it does give names of churches, dates andplaces which could be credible.

: The Southerns of Gilmer County [TN] can trace their ancestry back to
Jamestown, VA, when John Southern arrived there in 1620 in the ship"George". He was born in England ca 1600, the son of William Southern,hemarried '''Memory Tucker, the daughter of Capt. William Tucker'''. In1623, he
was a member of the House of Burgesses of Virginia and later he was
appointed acting secretary of the Colony.
We are descended from John and Memory Southern's son, John who marriedCatherine (surname unknown). They moved to Middlesex County, VA, wheretheywere members of Christ Episcopal Church, and for three genereations theirbirth, death and marriage record are recorded in the church records. JohnSouthern's will was made 10-3-1726 and proved 2-4-1728 in Middlesex County.
Their children were: Edward Southern who was baptized 5-25-1703; JohnSouthern, our ancestor, who was baptized 8-12-1705; William Southern and
Lettice Southern who married John Cheadle.
John Southern, the third generation, the son of John and Catherine,married 2-17-1720, Margaret Kidd, the daughter of William and MargaretKidd
of Middlesex County. The church records show that John died 21 October1759. All their children's births are recorded in the church records whenthey were baptized. They were: William Southern, our ancestor, baptized9-23-1722; Susanna Southern baptized 8-15-1725; Joseph, baptized 1-14-1727;
Benjamin baptized 10-28-1730.
