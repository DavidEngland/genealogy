=== Notes ===Family history states that Samuel's grave was desecrated by the British and Samuel's sons John and Thomas "took the horses to the swamp so that the British could not take them."
Samuel Hargrove was the clerk of the Cedar Creek Quaker Meeting at least during the years 1762, 1763, 1767, 1775, and 1777. He owned 190 acres of land, perhaps more.
