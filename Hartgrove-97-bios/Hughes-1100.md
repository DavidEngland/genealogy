== Biography ==
: "A Millicent Hughes was reported as a headright by Henry Jenkins when he patented land in Nansemond County in 1699.  There were the families of John Hughes and William Hughes living in Middlesex County before1679 and 1682 respectively. If Millicent was in the county that early, she would probably have been the daughter of John and Elizabeth Hughes. [[This is conjecture]] and based on the ages of their children."<ref name=Davis1>[[#S98612]]  Davis, p. 540</ref>
