==Biography==Mary Hodge was the daughter of  [[Hodges-606|John (Hodges) Hodge]] and[[Unknown-322865|Margaret (Unknown) Hunnicutt]]. <ref>Will of John Hodge: Surry Co VA Bk 2 Pg 212:</ref><ref>"Gillstrap-Anderson Genealogy"Hargrave, Bray, #7, [https://sites.google.com/site/gilstrapandersongenealogy/home/maternal-anderson-lines/hargrave Genealogy] (accessed [10May 2022])</ref>
She had a half sister, [[Cooper-6983|Elizabeth (Cooper) Rowell]], anda brother , James Hodge,  who died young.  She had three half siblings; Augustine Hunnicutt, Katherine Hunnicutt and Ann Hunnicutt; due to her mother's third marriage to [[Hunnicutt-89|Augustine Hunnicutt II/Jr]].
She married  [[Hargrave-54|Bray Hargrave]] sometime in the 1690s, Surry County, Virginia Colony. They had at least seven children:
#Benjamin
#Lemuel
#Elizabeth
#Augustine
#Margaret
#Samuel
#John

She died in 1769.
