== Biography ==
Elias Hardgrove married Patsey Gover, October 11, 1820 in Pulaski County, Kentucky. <ref>"Kentucky Marriages, 1785-1979," database, FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/F4H7-Q5B : 4 December 2014), Elias Hardgrove and Patsey Gover, 11 Oct 1820; citing Pulaski County, Kentucky, reference ; FHL microfilm 804,052.</ref>
He was listed in the 1860 census as Elis Hardgrove age 62  born Virginia living with his wife Martha, age 55, b KY and their family in La Plata Township, Macon County, Missouri. <ref>"United States Census, 1860", database with images, FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MHZZ-DNM : 30 December 2015), Elis Hardgrove, p 62. </ref>
