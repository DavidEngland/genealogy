== Biography ==Lemuel Hargrave was born about 1699.  He was the son of [[Hargrave-54|Bray Hargrave]] and [[Hodge-508|Mary (Hodge) Hargrave]].

He married [[Howell-915|Lydia Howell]] in 1728
Their children were,  Howell, James, Bray,  [[Hargrove-91|Charles Hargrove]], Margaret, and Jesse.
