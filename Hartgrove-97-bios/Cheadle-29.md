== Biography =="John Cheadle was living in Middlesex County by 1688.  It was recordedin the Christ Church Parish Register that he and Millicent Hughes were married on October 9, 1688. They were both living in Christ Church Parish at that time.  It is not known when John came into the colony; however, a John Cheadle was listed as a headright of Mr. Abraham Weeksand Mr. Hugh Williams on September 26, 1680 when they patented land onWare Creek on the back of land purchased by Henry Corbin."<ref>Davis,Virginia Lee Hutcheson. ''Tidewater Virginia Families.'' Urbanna, VA:self-published, 1989, pp. 539-540</ref>
When John was about 17 he married [[Hughes-1100|Millicent Hughes]] on9 October 1688 in Middlesex County, Virginia. The couple had 3 children: [[Cheadle-30|Thomas Cheadle]], [[Cheadle-10|John Cheadle]] and [[Cheadle-31|Hannah Cheadle]].<ref>
'''Marriage''': Christ Church, p. 37.
