== Biography ==
{{Quakers Sticker}}Millicent was born on 23 August 1773 in Caroline, Virginia, United States. She was the daughter of [[Hargrave-24|Samuel Hargrave Sr.]] and [[Cheadle-9|Martha (Cheadle) Hargrave]].
Millcent married Thomas Johnston on 6 March 1790 in Caroline County, Virginia.<ref>

Millicent died on 21 September 1844 in Lynchburg Virginia.
