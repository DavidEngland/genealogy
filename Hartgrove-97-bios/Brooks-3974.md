== Biography ==

Alice was born about 1685.
Married Henry Ball 2 Nov 1705 in Christchurch, Middlesex, Virginia.<ref>"Virginia Marriages, 1785-1940", database, FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:V299-7CG : 29 January 2020), Henery Ball,1705.</ref>

She passed away about 1714 in  Middlesex, Virginia
