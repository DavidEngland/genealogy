== Biography ==

===Probate===* Will dated Jan 12, 1769 and probated March 5, 1770, Amherst Co, Va.It listed his wife as Susanna and gave one shilling to eight persons -apparently his children - including one named Alice Wright. "In the Name of God - Amen. The twelfth Day of January in the Year of our LordMD CCLXIX, I, Valentine Ball of Amherst, Planter, being very sick andweak in Body but of perfect mind and memory, thanks be given unto Godfor the same, & Calling to mind the mortality of my Body and knowing that it is appointed for all men once to die, do make and ordain this my last Will and Testament. That is to say, Principally & first of allI Give and Recommend my Soul unto the hands of God that gave it and for my Body I Recommend it to the Earth to be Buried in a Christian like& Decent manner at the discreation of my Executors, nothing doubtingbut at the General Resurrection I shall receive the same again by themighty power of God , and as Touching such worldy Estate wherewith ithath pleased God to bless me in this life , I Give, Devise & Dispose of the Same in manner & form following, that is to Say In the first place after my Debts are paid I lend all & Singularmy whole Estate to Susanna Ball my well beloved wife during her natural life. I also give &bequeath to James Ball, Lewis Ball, Alice Wright, Sarah Bowman, SusanaHeughs, Elizabeth Heughs, John Ball & Mary Hargrove one Shilling Sterling each to be levied out of my Estate. I also Give to William Ball my well beloved Son the Land and Plantation whereon I now live after his mother's decease. I also give & bequeath to Jane Ball, my well beloved daughter, after her Mother's Decease, all and Singular my householdGoods of all kinds, Stock, Horses, Cattle, Sheep, Hogs & also the Negro Man named Will. I also give and bequeath to Capn Henry Key one fiveShills whom I also Constitute and Ordain with Susanna Ball Executor &Executrix of this my last Will and Testament and I do hereby utterlyDisallow Revoke and Disanul all and every other former Testaments, Wills & Legacies Requests & Executors by me in any ways before this timenamed, willed and bequeathed, Ratifying & Confirming this and no otherto be my last Will & Testament, In Witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and seal the Day and Year before written. Valentine Ball Signed, Sealed, Published, Pronounced & Declared by the said Valentine Ball as his last Will and Testament in presence of us the subscribers:Mary Key, Judith Tolley (their marks), William Bishnell"
