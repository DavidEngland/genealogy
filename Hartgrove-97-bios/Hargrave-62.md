== Biography =='''Benjamin Hargrave''' was probably born in Virginia (as his mother was born and died there, and his siblings were born there) c.1702, a son of [[Hargrave-54|Bray Hargrave]] (c.1668–<1728) and [[Hodge-508|Mary Hodge]] (<1679–1769).{{Citation Needed}}
Benjamin married '''[[Hancock-1220|Sarah Hancock]]''' (born about 1710in North Carolina). {{Citation Needed}}

Their children may have been:
* [[Hargrave-152|Mary Hargrave]] (1740–1811)* [[Hargrave-61|Benjamin Hargrave]] (c.1741–>1820) {{Citation Needed}}
Benjamin died about 1741, in the year his (possible) son, Benjamin, was born. {{Citation Needed}}
