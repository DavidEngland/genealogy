== Biography==[[Hunnicutt-17|Augustine Hunnicutt III]] was born to [[Hunnicutt-89|Augustine]] and [[Unknown-322865|Margaret Hunnicutt]] about 1684.
He married [[Newsom-235|Anne Newsom]], the daughter of William Newsomand Anne Sheppard.
Augustine Hunnicut died in Surry County, Virginia, after making his Will on 14 July 1743, and before it was proved on 19 October 1743.

In his Will, Augustine refers to his wife but does not name her. In addition to his wife, he makes bequests to the following named children:
* Robert, who has not reached the age of 21
* Augustine
* [[Hunnicutt-132|John]]
* Martha
* Mary

In addition, the Will identifies several slaves by name, including:
* Will
* Frank
* Betty
* Sary

Signed: Augustine Hunnicut (his mark).<br>
Executors: my sons John and Robert.<br>
Witnesses: William Seward.
