=== Birth &  Baptism ===
: Born 6 JUL 1723
: Baptized  21 JUL 1723
: Place:  Middlesex County, Virginia<ref>Christ Church, p. 113</ref>
=== Death ===
: Date:  1762
: Place:  Caroline County, Virginia: Division of his estate was recorded: Thomas Cheadle decedent, ThomasBankes, etc. administrators.: In 1767, Car MacGehee was named administrator. 1774, Elizabeth Cheadle admin.<ref>Caroline, Order Book, 1762, p. 286</ref>
=== Guardianship ===: The orphan children of Thomas Cheadle (George, Thomas, Sarah, Ursula, Judy, Lucy, Betsy, and Jacob) were named wards of John Minor. Theirguardianship was transferred to Henry Chiles in 1773<ref>Caroine, Order Book, 1762, p. 286</ref>
