== Biography ==Lemuel was the son of [[Hargrave-55|Richard Hargrave V]] and [[Pead-24|Pembroke (Pead)]]. <ref>https://sites.google.com/site/gilstrapandersongenealogy/home/maternal-anderson-lines/hargrave  Gilstrap Anderson Genealogy</ref>
Lemuel Hargrave died in Surry County, Virginia before 15 Sep 1742 whenhis will was probated. His will was made 2 May 1740.<ref name="Lems will"> Surry County, Virginia Will Book 9: 415-416, Lemuel Hargrave, ''FamilySearch''. [https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/3:1:3Q9M-C9PX-K99T?i=251&cat=366316] </ref><ref>"Bennet Boddie, John, ''Southside Virginia Families'', 1956, Vol 2, p. 134 [https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=wu.89062947890&view=1up&seq=150&skin=2021&q1=Hargrave HathiTrust] (accessed [13 Apr 2022])</ref>
Lemuel married Elizabeth Cornwell daughter of Samuel Cornwall and Catherine Hunnicutt.
