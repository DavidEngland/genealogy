== Biography ==Richard Hargrave was born circa 1500 in Sowerby Parish, Yorkshire, England.  He was a son of [[Hargrave-60|Nicolas Hargrave]]; his mother isnot known. {{Citation Needed}}
Richard died in October 1557 and was buried at Halifax on 18 October 1557 "Richard Hargreves de Sourby".<ref>The parish register of Halifax,part 2; Crossley, E. W., Publication date 1910, https://archive.org/details/parishregisterof45hali/page/199</ref> The date of 22 December 1557 previously given as his death date is the date of the grant of administration of his estate. Administration granted to son Richard Hargreaves.<ref>Halifax wills vol 2, J W Clay and E W Crossley, page 234,https://archive.org/details/halifaxwillsbein02york/page/234</ref>
