Bray, was apparently the first to use the name Hargrave. He moved withhis parents at age 13 to Surry County. He worked as a manager of a large plantation (Overseer), as did his father.

Bray was listed in the Virginia militia in 1687 (Col. Sur.-21).
No will has been found for Bray Hargrave. His estate was probated on 17 APR 1728. Some researchers show that his wife, [[Hodge-508|Mary (Hodge) Hargrave]] lived until 1769.  Bray Hargrave died Intestate January-February, 1728 in Surry County, Virginia. His wife Mary filed an inventory of his estate on 17 April, 1728.
