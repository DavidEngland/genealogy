== Biography ==

'''James Ervin "Ervie" White''' was born on '''8 July 1837''' in '''Kentucky, United States'''. He was commonly called '''"Ervie"''' by family and friends. He was the son of '''[[White-14189|Dempsey E White Sr.]]''' (age 39 at James's birth) and '''[[Perkins-3681|Nancy (Perkins) White]]''' (age 34 at James's birth).

James first married '''[[Matthews-2604|Christianna (Matthews) White]]'''. From this marriage he had two sons:
