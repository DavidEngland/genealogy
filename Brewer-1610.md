== Biography ==

'''Wiley Jackson Brewer''' was born in 1790 in '''North Carolina, United States'''. He died in 1868 in '''Wayne, Tennessee, United States'''.

Wiley married '''[[Cockman-17|Margaret Cockman]]''' in 1812 in '''North Carolina, United States'''. Margaret was born in 1794 in '''Moore, North Carolina, United States''' and died on '''7 November 1868''' in '''Wayne, Tennessee, United States'''.

* [[Brewer-1624|UNKNOWN Brewer]] (1820–?)

=== Parents ===

Wiley was the son of [[Brewer-708|Lanier B. Brewer, Jr.]] (1746–1824) and an unknown mother. Lanier was born in '''Brunswick, Colony of Virginia''' and died in '''Moore, North Carolina, United States'''.
