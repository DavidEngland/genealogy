== Biography ==

William Wesley Hargrove was born on 4 January 1816 in Alabama, United States. His father, [[Hargrove-## | Valentine Hargrove]], was 39 years old at the time, and his mother, [[Lewis-## | Ruth Whitley Lewis]], was 36.

He married Dicey Caruthers in 1837 in Tennessee, United States. Together they were the parents of at least 5 sons and 5 daughters.

William resided in Tippah, Mississippi, United States for about 11 years. In 1870, he was living in Mount Vernon Township, Lawrence, Missouri, United States.

He died in 1876 in Marion, Oregon, United States, at the age of 60.
