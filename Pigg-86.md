== Biography ==

'''John Henry Pigg''' was born about 1839-1840 in Tennessee. He married Martha Elvira N. Dalton on 9 August 1858 in Wayne County, Tennessee.<ref>Tennessee State Marriages, 1780-2002</ref> John served in the Union Army during the Civil War as part of Company E, 8th Tennessee Mounted Infantry.<ref>U.S., Civil War Pension Index: General Index to Pension Files, 1861-1934</ref>

John worked as a farmer in Wayne County, Tennessee throughout his life. He died in 1877 and is buried in Lawson Cemetery, Wayne County, Tennessee.<ref>Find a Grave, database and images (https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/95601275/john-henry-pigg: accessed 14 January 2024), memorial page for John Henry Pigg (1839–1877), Find a Grave Memorial ID 95601275, citing Lawson Cemetery, Wayne County, Tennessee, USA; Maintained by Charlotte Raley McConaha (contributor 47558988).</ref>

== Timeline ==

* '''c. 1839-1840''': Born in Tennessee
* '''9 August 1858''': Married Martha Elvira N. Dalton in Wayne County, Tennessee
* '''1860''': Living in District 10, Wayne County, with wife Elvira and infant son Henry; working as a farmer with $150 real estate and $200 personal property
* '''1861-1865''': Served in Company E, 8th Tennessee Mounted Infantry (Union Army)
* '''1870''': Living in District 10, Wayne County, with wife and seven children; owned $300 real estate and $600 personal estate
* '''1877''': Died in Wayne County, Tennessee; buried in Lawson Cemetery

== Research Notes ==

* Birth year discrepancy: Listed as "about 1840" in 1860 census (age 20) but "about 1839" in 1870 census (age 31) and on Find a Grave memorial
* Census records show John could not read or write
* His first son appears to be listed as "Henry" in early census records but later known as "William Henry"
