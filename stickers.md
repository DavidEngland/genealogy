WikiTree stickers are visual badges that highlight notable achievements or characteristics of ancestors. Here's how to use them effectively:

## Available Sticker Types

### 1. **US Southern Colonist Sticker**
For ancestors who settled in southern colonies before 1776:
```
{{US Southern Colonist Sticker|Virginia}}
{{US Southern Colonist Sticker|North Carolina}}
{{US Southern Colonist Sticker|South Carolina}}
```

### 2. **1776 Sticker** (Revolutionary War Service)
```
{{1776 Sticker|rank=Soldier|unit=Amherst County Militia, Virginia}}
{{1776 Sticker|rank=Captain|unit=Indiana Rangers}}
{{1776 Sticker|unit=Patriotic Service, Virginia, American Revolution}}
```

### 3. **Society Ancestor** (DAR/SAR Recognition)
```
{{Society Ancestor|society=DAR|ancestor=A084472}}
{{Society Ancestor|society=SAR|ancestor=P-123456}}
```

### 4. **Other Common Stickers**
- `{{Mayflower}}` - Mayflower descendants
- `{{War of 1812}}` - War of 1812 service
- `{{Pioneer}}` - Early frontier settlers
