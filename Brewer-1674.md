== Biography ==
George Washington Brewer was born in 1803 in Moore, North Carolina. He was the son of [[Brewer-953|Henry M. Brewer]] and [[Cockman-9|Mary (Cockman) Brewer]].<ref>"United States, Census, 1850", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MC63-BBP : Fri Mar 08 01:10:28 UTC 2024), Entry for George W Brewer and Harriet M Brewer, 1850.</ref>

He married [[Wilson-54405|Elizabeth (Wilson) Brewer]] in 1829 in Wayne, Tennessee, and later married [[Whitten-526|Harriet Meranday (Whitten) Brewer]] about 1844.<ref>"United States, Census, 1850", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MC63-BBP : Fri Mar 08 01:10:28 UTC 2024), Entry for George W Brewer and Harriet M Brewer, 1850.</ref>

In 1850 he appears in Wayne County, Tennessee with his household, including children Lewis A., Martha J., Nancy E., James H., and Richard.<ref>"United States, Census, 1850", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MC63-BBP : Fri Mar 08 01:10:28 UTC 2024), Entry for George W Brewer and Harriet M Brewer, 1850.</ref>

George died about 1852 in Wayne County, Tennessee, and was buried there.<ref>Ancestry.com: Tennessee, Wills and Probate Records, 1727-2008, "1852-09-06 - Inventory and Account of Sales of the Effects of G. W. Brewer" (https://sg30p0.familysearch.org/service/records/storage/dascloud/patron/v2/TH-904-57717-804-0/thumb200.jpg?ctx=ArtCtxPublic : accessed Sep 2, 2015).</ref>
