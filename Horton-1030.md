== Biography ==
'''[[Horton-1030|Hannah (Horton) Brewer]]''' was born on 24 February 1828 in Wayne County, Tennessee. Her father, [[Horton-1013|Nathaniel Ananias Horton]], was 38 and her mother, [[Atkinson-1311|Malinda "Millie" (Atkinson) Horton]], was 27. She married '''[[Brewer-1601|Rial (Ryal/Riley) Brewer, Esq.]]''' in 1842 in Wayne County, Tennessee. They had nine children in 18 years. 

Her husband Rial was born in North Carolina and served as High Sheriff of Wayne County. He died in Louisville, Kentucky, in 1862. Hannah remained in Wayne County, Tennessee, where she appears as head of household in the 1870, 1880, and 1900 census records. She died on 5 October 1899 in Wayne County, Tennessee, at the age of 71, and was buried in Horton Cemetery.
