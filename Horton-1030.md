== Biography ==
'''Hannah (Horton) Brewer''' was born on 24 February 1828 in Wayne County, Tennessee. Her father, [[Horton-1013|Nathaniel Ananias Horton]], was 38 and her mother, [[Atkinson-1311|Malinda "Millie" (Atkinson) Horton]], was 27. She died on 5 October 1899 in Wayne County, Tennessee, at the age of 71, and was buried in Wayne County.

She married '''[[Brewer-1601|Rial (Ryal/Riley) Brewer, Esq.]]''' before '''1844''' in Tennessee (born in North Carolina; High Sheriff of Wayne County; died in Louisville, Kentucky, in 1862).

She lived in Tennessee in 1870 and in Civil District 7, Wayne County, Tennessee, in 1900.
