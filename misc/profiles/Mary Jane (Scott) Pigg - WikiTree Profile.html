I added the parents because I found it on FamilySearch.Org. I am not 100% convinced that it is true, although I would like it to be for the rich family history. Perhaps it will take DNA testing to help. I am also including

Here is what I found on this John Martin

John Martin Scott was born 1791 in Wayne Co Tn died in Wayne and is in/t Jacob son? cem married Mary Cynthia White she was born in Ky children are

  • 1..William M Scott born May 12 1839 Wayne Co maried Sarah Jane Pigg in 1862 Wm is i/t McGlamery Cem Collinwood Tn this couple had five ch Martha, George, Henry, John M , Dock
  • 2..Frederick M Scott born 1833
  • 3..John James Scott born Feb 10 1838
  • 4..Mary Jane Scott born Dec 23 1842 she married Wm M Pigg Oct 1861 seven children died Sept 1907
  • 5..Madison Coulumbus Scott born Nov 1836 married Elizabeth A Gray Mar 1870
  • 6..Eliza Scott born 1848
  • 7..Franklin Scott born Jun 1849 married June 1876 Elvisa Cady in Wayne Co moved to McNairy Co Tn where he died Oct 1821
  • 8..Isabella Scott born 1851 maried George W Gray Sept 1868

I think John Martin Scott that was born 1791 he married Anna unk one son Samuel Scott born 1823 and died 1905 Samuel married Lydia Keeton in Wayne Co Tn This John Martin was the son of James and Rebecca Scott of North Carolina.

I think these Scotts are kin to Hariam Scott born Feb 1794 in South Carolina he married a Lucretia Tidwell ....Hiriam Scott moved to Pickens Alabama in/t Unity Cem He was the son of Benjamin Scott born Sept 25 1756 married  ? Margaret ? he had eight chidren Hiram, Peggy, Eliz, Daniel, Fanny , Nancy , Susannah , Jane

John Martin B Scott born 1791 in Va moved to Wayne Co Tn died there in 1870 he was the son of Wm Scott and Corenna Green he married Mary Cynthia White, Anna Eliz Snapp Cynthia is living with her youngest son Frank in 1880 census she died in McNairy Co Tn and is buried there near the family of her son Franklin P Scott on Granny Scott Hollow at 48 mile Creek in Wayne Co This is said to be named after her John Martin Scott was the first child of Jame and Rebecca, they first settled in Williamson Co Tn prior to Wayne Co sometiem before 1820 He filed for Rev War Tract of 200ac his first wife Anna Elizabeth died ab 1829 November 18 1844 John Martin Scott deeds a sorrel mare to his nephew Wm P Jackson son of Nancy James Scott b 1765 NC came to Wayne Co bef 1820 settled on 48 mile Creek east of Wayne Co Tn In 1850 Rebecca is living with her Barnabus and Mary Jackson ( her daughter )


George White was a early settler in Wayne Co he had a farm a few hundred yards sw of JN Davis land. Soome of the other settlers was Coopers , Sims (Joseph ) he was the son in law of Davis also in the early settlers was a William Scott and Thomas Martin along with Lindsey , Robersons and Nowlins ,Gallaher , Parkers Dickerson , Davis, Thomapson own Martins Mills , Yates and Walkers.

John Martin Scott was a Magistrate in Wayne Co Tn.

The early Scott names listed for

1830 in Wayne

James , James, James Sr, John , John Sr, John M ,Rosamond P

Scotts in 1820 Wayne Tn

Berry, Jame, Jeremiah, John, Joseph, Martin, William F, Adams Scott (notice Martin Scott)


In 1810 we have two Scotts Moses and Reubin in Rutherford Tn I would say that the Wayne Co Scott could be kin to the Bedford Tn Scott
