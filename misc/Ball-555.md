== Biography ==

Mary (Ball) Hargrove was born about 1749 in Middlesex, Virginia, United States, to Valentine Ball (age 44) and Susannah (Lewis) Ball (age 33). She married '''John Washington Hargrove Sr.''' about 1772 in Middlesex, Virginia, United States. They were the parents of at least eleven children (5 sons and 6 daughters), including:
* William H. Hargrove
* Valentine Hargrove
* John W. (or Lindsey) Hargrove Jr.
* James B. Hartgrove
* Lindsey Hargrove

She died about 1813 in Pulaski, Pulaski County, Kentucky, United States, at about age 66.
