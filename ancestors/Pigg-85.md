== Biography ==

'''John Henry Pigg I''' (1817–1879) was born in 1817 in Tennessee, United States. He was the son of '''[[Pigg-116|John Nathan Pigg]]''' (1765–1835) and '''[[Cate-181|Sarah Callie Cate]]''' (1781–1875).

=== Marriage ===
Married '''[[Gray-5898|Eliza Jane Gray]]''' in 1837 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States. She was born 1817 and died 1896.

Note: An earlier marriage to '''[[Cate-290|Mary J. Cate]]''' is referenced in some records but appears to predate the documented marriage to Eliza Jane Gray.
