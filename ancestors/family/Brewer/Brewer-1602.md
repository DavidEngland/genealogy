== Biography ==

'''Sarah Jane Brewer''' was born on 17 February 1847 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States.<ref name="birth1847">"United States, Census, 1850", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MC63-H3H : accessed 17 Jan 2025), Entry for Sarah Brewer in household of Riley Brewer and Hannah Brewer, 1850.</ref> She was the daughter of [[Brewer-1601|Rial (Ryal/Riley) Brewer]] (1817–1862) and [[Horton-1030|Hannah (Horton) Brewer]] (1828–1899).

Her father, Rial Brewer, served as '''High Sheriff and Tax Collector''' of Wayne County beginning in 1850. He later served in the '''10th Regiment Infantry (1st Middle Tennessee Infantry)''' of the Union Army during the Civil War, reflecting Wayne County's strong pro-Union stance. He died on 13 November 1862 in Louisville, Kentucky.

=== Marriage ===
Sarah married [[Lawson-1614|George Washington Lawson]] on 26 July 1866 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States.<ref name="marriage1866">"Tennessee, County Marriages, 1790-1959", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:Q2Z9-XJXP : accessed 15 Feb 2025), Entry for George W Lawson and Sarah Brewer, 26 Jul 1866.</ref>

=== Residences ===
Sarah and her family lived in Martins Mills, Wayne County, Tennessee, United States in 1910<ref name="census1910">"United States, Census, 1910", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MGNV-6QW : accessed 14 Aug 2025), Entry for George W Lawson and Sarah J Lawson, 1910.</ref> and Civil District 4, Wayne County, Tennessee, United States in 1920.<ref name="census1920">"United States, Census, 1920", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MN55-3L7 : accessed 22 Oct 2025), Entry for George Lawson, Sr and Sarah Lawson, 1920.</ref>

=== Death ===
Sarah Jane died on 13 March 1923 in Collinwood, Wayne County, Tennessee, United States, at the age of 76. She was buried in Cromwell Crossroads Cemetery, Collinwood, Wayne County, Tennessee, United States.<ref>{{FindAGrave|135928719}}</ref>

Her mother, '''Hannah (Horton) Brewer''', is buried at Horton Cemetery in Wayne County, Tennessee.
