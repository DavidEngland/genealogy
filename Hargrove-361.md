== Biography ==

'''Lydia Serena "Liddie" (Hargrove) White''' was born on 19 June 1854 in Tennessee, the daughter of '''[[Hargrove-287|James Monroe Hargrove]]''' and '''[[Welch-1878|Serena Welch]]'''.

=== Marriage ===
She married '''James Ervin White''' on 1 October 1877 in Hardin County, Tennessee.

=== Children ===
They had at least seven children:
* '''[[White-14223|Mary Elinor (White) Alexander]]'''
* '''[[White-14260|Sarah Inez White]]'''
* '''[[White-14262|Benjamin White]]'''
* '''[[White-14265|Edward Martin White]]'''
* '''[[White-14258|Lawerence E. White]]'''
* '''[[White-14263|Bessie (White) Alexander]]'''
* '''[[White-14146|Myrtle (White) Whitten]]'''

=== Death and Burial ===
Lydia died on 10 March 1897 in Hardin County, Tennessee, and was buried in '''Gillis Cemetery''', Hardin County, Tennessee.
