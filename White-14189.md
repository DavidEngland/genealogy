== Biography ==
''Member of the Kentucky Legislature?''

Born '''1798''' in '''South Carolina, United States''', son of '''[[White-12146|John Calvin White Sr.]]''' (age 45) and '''Mary E. Norris''' (age 36). Half-sibling to Richard, Hendricks, George, Charlotta, Harrison, Mary, John Calvin Jr., and Nicholas A. White (per Whitley County records).

'''Dempsey (Dempsy, Demcy) White''' is not as an uncommon name as one might first suspect!

On 24 January 1819 in Whitley County, Kentucky, he married '''[[Perkins-3681|Nancy Perkins]]'''. They were the parents of at least 6 sons and 5 daughters, including:
* [[White-14308|Jabes White]]
* [[White-14277|Jesse Perkins White Sr.]]
* [[White-14276|Milton E. White]]
* [[White-14310|Dempsy Lawson White M.D.]]
* [[White-14309|Emily (White) Mahan]]
* [[White-14307|Louisa J. White]]
* [[White-14147|James Ervin "Ervie" White]]
* [[White-14274|Nancy (White) Parker]]
* [[White-14311|Mary A (White) Lawson]]

Died in '''September 1859''' in '''Wayne County, Tennessee''', at about age 61.
