== Biography ==

'''Sarah Tapley Clark''' (often recorded as "Sally" or "Aunt Sallie") was born on 23 February 1806 in Tennessee, United States. She was the daughter of '''Robert Clark''' (age 28 at her birth) and '''Lucy Farmer''' (age 22 at her birth). On 27 March 1829, Sarah married '''Moses Duncan''' in Lauderdale, Alabama, United States. Sarah and Moses had at least 4 sons and 5 daughters. She lived in Florence, Lauderdale, Alabama in 1870. Sarah died on 6 June 1894 in Lauderdale, Alabama, United States, at the age of 88 years, and was buried in Lauderdale, Alabama, United States.

'''Note on Name:''' Sarah's middle name is variously recorded as '''Tapley''' or '''Tarpley''' in historical documents.
