== Biography ==

'''Malisia Catherine Lawson''' was born on '''29 July 1883''' in '''Wayne, Tennessee, United States'''. She was also known as '''Melissa''' and by the nickname '''Lisse'''. She was the daughter of '''[[Lawson-1614|George Washington Lawson]]''' and '''[[Brewer-1602|Sarah Jane Brewer]]'''.

=== Marriage ===

She married '''[[Bridgers-26|James Howard Bridgers]]''' on '''24 May 1907''' in '''Wayne, Tennessee, United States'''. James was born on '''26 February 1884''' in '''Montgomery County, Tennessee, United States''' and died on '''14 January 1953''' in '''Sumner, Lamar County, Texas, United States'''.

=== Children ===

Malisia and James were the parents of at least 6 sons and 4 daughters.

=== Residences ===

* '''1930''' - Rock Creek, Colbert, Alabama, United States
* '''1940''' - Muse Township, Le Flore, Oklahoma, United States

=== Death ===

She died on '''27 November 1962''' in '''Paris, Lamar, Texas, United States''', at the age of '''79'''. She was buried in '''Little Vine Cemetery, Globe, Lamar County, Texas, United States'''.
