== Biography ==
Paul was born to the parents of John Whitley and Ervie Mitchell.
Paul Whitley married Jane Kirkham (1744-1765).  They had at least twochildren; Robert (1756-1802), and Samuel (1762-1830).
Paul Whitley was among those recruited by Benjamin Burden (or Borden)in Great Britain ca. 1737 to colonize Burden's land grant in Virginia.  Paul settled on Cedar Creek in what would later be Augusta Co, VIrginia.
Paul Whitley served on Gen. Braddock's expedition in a rifle company from Augusta Co, Virginia,  commanded by Capt. Samuel Lewis.
