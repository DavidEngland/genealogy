== Biography ==

'''John Calvin White Sr.''' was born about '''1768''' in '''Bute County, North Carolina, United States'''. He was the son of '''[[White-12147|Richard White Jr.]]''' and '''[[Hudson-2367|Elizabeth Hudson]]'''.

John Calvin White Sr. was a pivotal figure in the migration of the White family from North Carolina to the Kentucky and Tennessee frontiers. He became foundational to the settlement of '''Whitley County, Kentucky''', where he established extensive land and legal interests that would be managed by his heirs for generations.

=== Children ===
John had at least nine children from his marriages:
* '''[[White-12194|Richard White]]'''
* '''[[White-12195|Hendricks White]]'''
* '''[[White-12197|George White]]'''
* '''[[White-12196|Charlotta (White) Smith]]'''
* '''[[White-12198|Harrison White]]'''
* '''[[White-12199|Mary White]]'''
* '''[[White-12145|John Calvin White Jr.]]'''
* '''[[White-14189|Dempsey E White]]''' (b. 1798 South Carolina) – son who inherited his father's civic-mindedness, serving as a member of the '''Kentucky Legislature''' before settling in the '''Wayne County''' area. Dempsey continued to manage the extensive land and legal interests established by his father.
* '''[[White-12200|Nicholas A. White]]'''

His children, including Dempsey, Richard, and Harrison, carried the family legacy into the Deep South and became foundational to settlements throughout the region.

=== Death ===
John Calvin White Sr. died about '''1830''' in '''Whitley County, Kentucky, United States'''. His death marked the transition of the family's leadership to his son Dempsey, who continued to manage the family's affairs and legal interests.
