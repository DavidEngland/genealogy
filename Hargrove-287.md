== Biography ==

'''James Monroe Hargrove''' (1819–1884) was born on '''6 May 1819''' in '''Alabama''', United States.<ref name="census1850">"United States, Census, 1850", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MCDN-55D : accessed 18 Jan 2025), Entry for James M Hargrove and Lydia A Hargrove, 1850.</ref> He was the son of '''[[Hargrove-277|Valentine Hargrove]]''' (c. 1778–1858) and '''[[Lewis-8883|Ruth Whitley Lewis]]''' (1791–). At the time of his birth, his father was 42 years old and his mother was 39.

=== Siblings ===

He was one of at least ten children born to Valentine and Ruth, including: '''[[Hargrove-292|Mary Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-289|Ruth Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-284|Elizabeth Louisiana Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-285|Samuel Ball Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-293|Sarah Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-286|William Wesley Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-288|Omar Hassen Hargrove]]''', '''[[Hargrove-290|John Lindsey Hargrove]]''', and '''[[Hargrove-291|Jackson Hargrove]]'''.

=== First Marriage and Family ===

James Monroe married a woman named '''Lydia A.''' (maiden name uncertain; possibly '''Poole''') around '''1850'''.<ref name="census1850" /> Lydia was documented in the 1850 census as '''Lydia A Hargrove''' but her maiden name requires verification from primary sources (marriage record, family documents, etc.).

Together, James Monroe and Lydia had '''at least two children''':

* '''Ruth Elizabeth Hargroves''' (b. c. 1849)
* '''William Valentine Hargrove''' (1851–1937)

Lydia died in '''1851''', likely from childbirth complications.

=== Second Marriage and Family ===

James Monroe married '''[[Welch-1878|Serena (Welch) Hargrove]]''' about '''1853''' in '''Tennessee''', United States.<ref name="marriage1853">"Tennessee Marriage Records", database, FamilySearch (accessed 1853), Entry for James Monroe Hargrove and Serena Welch.</ref> Serena was the daughter of '''Elijah C. Welch''' (1810–1867) and '''Mary Taylor''' (1801–1880).

Together, James Monroe and Serena had '''at least seven children''': at least two sons and five daughters born between approximately 1853 and the 1870s.<ref name="census1880" />

* '''[[Hargrove-?|Lydia Serena Hargrove]]''' (1854–1897) – Married '''[[White-523|James Ervin "Ervie" White]]'''
* '''[[Hargrove-?|Mary Wright Hargrove]]''' (1856–1935)
* '''[[Hargrove-?|Minerva Jane Hargrove]]''' (c. 1859–1928)
* '''[[Hargrove-?|Martha Hargrove]]''' (c. 1862–)
* '''[[Hargrove-?|Margaret Alice Hargrove]]''' (c. 1864–)
* '''[[Hargrove-?|James Columbus Hargrove]]''' (1866–1938)
* '''[[Hargrove-?|Lorenzo Dow Hargrove]]''' (c. 1868–)

Serena died in '''1884''', the same year as James Monroe.
