== Biography ==
Roxie May England, daughter of [[England-1361|William Eldredge England]] and [[McDonald-4057|Palina Altimira (McDonald) England]], was born 8 May 1887. She married, as his second wife, [[McClure-610|John Lafayette McClure]], (1866-1912) on 30 October 1907. They had one son, [[McClure-613|Emmett McClure]]. Roxie May England died 8 Apr 1907.
