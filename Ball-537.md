Biography

**Note:** Ball-537 is identified as Henry "H" Ball (c. 1676–1735), son of the immigrant Henry Ball. He signed documents with an "H" to distinguish himself from his father. First appeared as an adult in 1697 in Middlesex County records. He resided at Ball's Neck in Christ Church Parish, upper Middlesex County, Virginia. Father of Valentine Ball (1706–1770). *(TAG, Vol. 78, No. 3 & 4)*

Married Alice Brooks 2 Nov 1705 in Christchurch, Middlesex, Virginia.[1]
