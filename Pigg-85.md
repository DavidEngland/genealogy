== Biography ==

'''John Henry Pigg I''' (1817–1879) was born in 1817 in Williamson County, Tennessee, United States. He was the son of '''[[Pigg-116|John Nathan Pigg]]''' (1765–1835) and '''[[Cate-181|Sarah Callie Cate]]''' (1781–1875).

=== Marriage ===
Married '''[[Gray-5898|Eliza Jane Gray]]''' on 1837 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States. Eliza Jane Gray was born 1817 and died 1896.

=== Children ===
John Henry and Eliza Jane Pigg were the parents of at least 10 children:

* '''[[Pigg-85|John Henry Pigg]]''' (b. 1839, d. 1876) — married Martha Elvira Dalton
* '''[[Pigg-87|Sarah Jane Pigg]]''' (b. 1840, d. 1921)
* '''[[Pigg-84|William M. Pigg]]''' (b. 1842, d. 1888) — married Mary Jane Scott on 31 October 1861
* '''[[Pigg-89|Lucinda Caroline Pigg]]''' (b. 1845, d. 1926)
* '''[[Pigg-90|Susannah Pigg]]''' (b. 1846) — spouse unknown
* '''[[Pigg-91|Mary Frances Pigg]]''' (b. 1849, d. 1937)
* '''[[Pigg-88|James Henry Pigg]]''' (b. 1851, d. 1897)
* '''[[Pigg-92|Jerry Henderson Pigg]]''' (b. 1853, d. 1936) — married Malinda Jane Martin
* '''[[Pigg-93|George W[ashington/Marshall] Pigg]]''' (b. 1860, d. 1942)

=== Death ===
John Henry died on April 1879 in Cypress Inn, Wayne County, Tennessee, United States, and was buried in Lawson Cemetery, Wayne County, Tennessee.
