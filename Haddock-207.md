== Biography ==

'''Rachel Emily Haddock''' was born in 1838 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States.<ref name="census1860">"United States, Census, 1860", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M8TY-2L8 : accessed 8 Jul 2024), Entry for William B Parish and Rachael E Parish, 1860.</ref><ref name="findagrave1">{{FindAGrave|18123562}}</ref> She was the daughter of '''James E Haddock Jr''' (c. 1795–) and '''Acenah Paulk''' (c. 1798–). At the time of her birth, her father was 43 years old and her mother was 40.

=== Marriage and Family ===

Rachel married '''Brown Parish''' in 1854 in Wayne County, Tennessee, United States.<ref name="marriage1854">"Tennessee, County Marriages, 1790-1959", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:Q2Z9-XJXL : accessed 15 Feb 2025), Entry for Rachel Haddock and Brown Parish, 1854.</ref> Together they had at least eight sons and seven daughters.

=== Residences ===

Rachel and her family lived in various locations throughout their lives:
* 1860 – Wayne County, Tennessee<ref name="census1860" />
* 1870 – Wayne County, Tennessee<ref name="census1870">"United States, Census, 1870", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:MD88-7V3 : accessed 13 Oct 2025), Entry for W B Parish and Rachel E Parish, 1870.</ref>
* 1880 – Wayne County, Tennessee<ref name="census1880">"United States, Census, 1880", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M4JP-7BJ : accessed 20 Jan 2025), Entry for William Parish and Rachel Parish, 1880.</ref>
* 1900 – Lauderdale County, Alabama<ref name="census1900">"United States, Census, 1900", FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M9D3-F3V : accessed 22 Oct 2025), Entry for Rachiel E Parish and Alice F Parish, 1900.</ref> (postal address: Oakland; formerly Cloverdale, Tennessee)

Rachel and Brown Parish relocated from Wayne County, Tennessee to Lauderdale County, Alabama during the later years of their marriage. During this relocation, their postal address changed from Cloverdale to Oakland. Rachel was instrumental in the establishment of '''New Hope Church of Christ''', having donated the land upon which the church was built.

=== Death and Burial ===

Rachel Emily Haddock died on 19 February 1907 in Florence, Lauderdale County, Alabama, United States, at the age of 69.<ref name="findagrave1" /><ref name="findagrave2">{{FindAGrave|140514866}}</ref> She was buried at '''Pleasant Hill United Methodist Church Cemetery''' in Florence, Lauderdale County, Alabama, United States.
