== Biography ==

Eliza Ann Pigg was born in 1881 in Wayne, Tennessee, United States, the daughter of William T. Pigg and Mary Jane Scott. She married George Henry Lawson on 21 December 1902 in Wayne, Tennessee, United States. They were the parents of at least nine children. She died in 1969 in Cherokee, Alabama, United States and was buried in Tri-Cities Memorial Gardens, Florence, Lauderdale County, Alabama.
