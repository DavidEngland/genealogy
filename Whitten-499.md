== Biography ==

'''Robert Duncan "Bob" Whitten''' (WikiTree ID: [[Whitten-499]]) was born on '''7 February 1861''' in Lauderdale County, Alabama. He was the son of '''[[Whitten-500|Jesse P. Whitten]]''' (WikiTree ID: [[Whitten-500]]) (1827â€“1882) and '''[[Duncan-3524|Martha E. (Duncan) Whitten]]''' (WikiTree ID: [[Duncan-3524]]) (b. 1832). 

At the time of Robert's birth, his father Jesse was 34 years old and his mother Martha was 28. Robert carried the Duncan middle name in tribute to his mother's Scottish heritage and Aberdeen ancestry.

=== Marriage and Children ===

Robert married '''[[Parish-378|Laroca Francis Parish]]''' on '''19 December 1883''' in Lauderdale County, Alabama, at age 22. Together they had two sons:
* '''[[Whitten-502|William N. Whitten]]'''
* '''[[Whitten-498|Early Whitten]]'''

=== Life in Lauderdale County ===

Robert and Laroca lived near where the Central Heights Little League ball field is today in Lauderdale County. In 1930, Robert was residing in Oakland, Lauderdale, Alabama.

My neighbor and distant cousin Elvis Grissom '''a.k.a.''' Gresham told me that he'd walk by Bob Whitten's and he'd say, "Come here boy". He'd have Elvis shave him with a straight razor. Elvis said that his whiskers were like saw briers.

=== Death ===

Robert Duncan "Bob" Whitten passed away on '''8 February 1937''' in Lauderdale County, Alabama, one day after his 76th birthday.
