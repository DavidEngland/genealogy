== Biography ==

Newell was born in 1826. Newell England ...  He passed away in 1909. <ref>Entered by Jeff Kenner, Jul 17, 2012</ref>

Born 4 February 1826 in Bedford County, Tennessee; son of John England and Elizabeth Roberts.<ref>Roberts Family Bible; FamilySearch “Family Tree” PID LDRG-PZQ.</ref>
Married Martha Ann Glidewell (indexed as Chadwell/Glidewell) on 3 December 1843 in Lincoln County, Tennessee.<ref>"Tennessee, County Marriages, 1790-1959," FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:Q2Z9-CYQJ).</ref>
They were parents of at least 6 sons and 4 daughters, based on 1850/1860/1880/1900 census enumerations.<ref>1850: https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M4LN-D8Q; 1860: https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M8WM-SXL; 1880: https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M6FM-3LZ; 1900: https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M3XQ-JJG.</ref>
Residence in 1850: Tippah County, Mississippi (age 23), household with Martha A. England, Henry M. England, Thomas R. England, and John B. England; birthplace Tennessee.<ref>"United States Census, 1850," FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:M4LN-D8Q).</ref>
Family oral history suggests Newell may have taken in one of his brother Jonathan’s children (Elizabeth b. 1838, William b. 1840, John Milton b. 1841, Jane b. 1842, or Jonathan b. 1844); not believed to be William or Jane—Jane is reported to have gone with Martin to AR, then LA, and married in TX.<ref>Family oral history (England family tradition).</ref>
Died 14 August 1909 in Elm Store, Randolph County, Arkansas; buried in Randolph County, Arkansas.<ref>Find A Grave Index, FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6V7H-RHLF).</ref>
