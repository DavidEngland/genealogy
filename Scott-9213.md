John Martin Scott was a Magistrate in Wayne Co Tn  

John Martin B Scott born 1791 in Va moved to Wayne Co Tn died there in 1870  

Cynthia is living with her youngest son Frank in 1880 census she died in McNairy Co Tn and is buried there near the family of her son Franklin P Scott  on Granny Scott Hollow at 48 mile Creek in Wayne Co This is said to be named after her  John Martin Scott was the first child of James and Rebecca , they first settled in Williamson Co Tn prior to Wayne Co  sometime before 1820 He filed for Rev War Tract of 200ac his first wife Anna Elizabeth died ab 1829  November 18 1844 John Martin Scott deeds a sorrel mare to his nephew Wm P Jackson son of Nancy

His first wife Anna Elizabeth Carrell OR Snapp died ab 1829

I think John Martin Scott that was born 1791 he married Anna unk one son Samuel Scott born 1823 and died  1905 Samuel married Lydia Keeton in Wayne Co Tn This John Martin was the son of James and Rebecca Scott of North Carolina
I think these Scotts are kin to Hariam Scott born Feb 1794 in South Carolina  he married a Lucretia Tidwell ....Hiriam Scott moved to Pickens Alabama  in/t Unity Cem He was the son of Benjamin Scott born Sept 25 1756  married  ? Margaret ?  he had  eight children Hiram , Peggy, Eliz, Daniel, Fanny , Nancy , Susannah , Jane

James Scott b 1765 NC came to Wayne Co bef 1820 settled on 48 mile Creek east of Wayne Co Tn  In 1850 Rebecca is living with her Barnabus and Mary Jackson ( her daughter )

George White was a early settler in Wayne Co he had a farm a few hundred yards sw of JN Davis land  some of the other settlers was Coopers , Sims (Joseph )  he was the son in law of Davis also in the early settlers was a William Scott and Thomas Martin  along with Lindsey , Robersons and Nowlins ,Gallaher , Parkers Dickerson , Davis, Thomapson own Martins Mills , Yates and Walkers

Early Scott names in 1820 Wayne Tn Berry, Jame, Jeremiah, John, Joseph, Martin, William F, Adams Scott  (notice Martin Scott)

Early Scott  names listed for 1830 in Wayne James , James, James Sr, John , John Sr, John M ,Rosamond P
