== Biography ==

'''Sarah Tapley Clark''' was born on 6 February 1806 in North Carolina, United States.<ref name="birth">Legacy NFS Source: Individual or family possessions</ref> She was the daughter of [[Clark-XXXX|Robert Clark]] and [[Farmer-XXXX|Lucy Farmer]].

On 27 March 1829, Sarah married [[Duncan-XXXX|Moses Duncan]] in Alabama.<ref name="marriage1">"Alabama, Marriages, 1816-1957," database, FamilySearch (https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:FQV5-BL7 : accessed 13 February 2020), Moses Duncan and Sally Clark, 1829.</ref><ref name="marriage2">"Alabama County Marriages, 1711-1992," database, FamilySearch (https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:Q2DQ-TKGX : accessed 22 August 2025), Moses Duncan and Sally Clark, 27 Mar 1829.</ref>

Sarah and Moses had at least ten children, including [[Duncan-3524|Martha E. Duncan]] who married [[Whitten-500|Jesse P. Whitten]].

Sarah passed away about 1894-1896 in Florence, Lauderdale County, Alabama, United States.<ref name="death1">Legacy NFS Source: Individual or family possessions; death about 1896</ref><ref name="findagrave">"Find a Grave Index," database, FamilySearch (https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:QK1V-B5PK : accessed 3 April 2025), entry for Aunt Sallie.</ref>
